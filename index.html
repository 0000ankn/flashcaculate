Flash Calculator Support Page

About the App

Flash Calculator is a lightweight app designed to help you practice mental arithmetic quickly and easily.

  • Custom difficulty (choose digits and calculation speed)
  • Answer input
  • Warm and minimal clean design

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1. Can I use the app without internet access?
A. Yes, all practice features work offline.

Q2. How do I adjust the difficulty level?
A. On the main screen, you can directly select digits and speed to set your preferred difficulty.
